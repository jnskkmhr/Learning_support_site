TIPS FOR LEARNING

Even though you put a lot of time on studying and reviewing, some of you might have experienced that you do not see as much progress as you expected. The possible cause of this is that you do not know HOW TO STUDY. This site give you a big idea of how to study or where to start for each subject.

SELF-STUDY

With enough resources and tips for studying, you are recommended to study by yourself. One reason for self-study is that you can think deeply at your own pace. Through deep thinking, you can train your ability to think deeply. Another reason is that you cannot always rely on teachers and wait for them to teach you everything. Of cource, it is perfectly fine that you ask what you do not understand, but you are highly recommended to switch from tottaly relying on teachers to modifying your schedule and putting effort to fulfill your short-term or long-term goals.
