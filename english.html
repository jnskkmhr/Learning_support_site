English

Learn Support provides resources and original guide for English Learner
Under Preparation!

I. Reading

1. Khan Academy

Kphan Academy provides a lot of free resources for English examination such as SAT and ACT (Both of these are used for university application in mainly the United States and the other countries.)

HARD

2. National Geography

It might be hard to read for beginners, but if you are pretty interested in the newest science, it might be worth trying to read. A variety of vocabularies and grammars are used in the article, so you can learn a lot just by taking note of them and try to use them in your own essay or composition.

HARD ~ INTERMIDIATE

3. TOEFL ITP Textbook

Toefl is language proficiency test offered by educational organization in the U.S. called ETS. English used in Toefl ITP is not so high (that's not to say this is easy). Thus, it might be fine for your reading practice. A variety of topic (geology, astronomy, botany, zoology, linguistics, history etc) is dicussed in the test, so you would be able to learn a variety of vocabularies just by reading passages.

INTERMIDIATE

II. Listening

1. NETFLIX Drama

NETFLIX is a subscription-based movie broadcasting platform. You can watch movies and TV-series as well as NETFLIX original movie. English used in drama or movies is quite hard to understand since pharases used in these are idomatic and informal. Additionally, people speak so fast that you cannot catch a lot of phrases. However, you still can learn a lot from English subtitle and you can use in your converstion.
